here's the key infomation on the movie

The Devil Wears Prada is a 2006 American comedy-drama film directed by David Frankel and produced by Wendy Finerman. The screenplay, written by Aline Brosh McKenna, is based on the 2003 novel by Lauren Weisberger. The film stars Meryl Streep, Anne Hathaway, Stanley Tucci, and Emily Blunt.

The story follows Andy Sachs (Hathaway), an aspiring journalist who lands a job at a prestigious fashion magazine. However, she soon finds herself working under the powerful and demanding editor-in-chief, Miranda Priestly (Streep).

20th Century Fox purchased the rights to adapt the novel in 2003, even before it was completed. The project was officially approved after Meryl Streep agreed to star in the film. Filming lasted 57 days, primarily in New York City between October and December 2005, with additional scenes shot in Paris.

The film premiered at the LA Film Festival on June 22, 2006, and was released in the United States on June 30, 2006. It received positive reviews, especially praising Streep’s performance.

Streep won the Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Motion Picture Musical or Comedy and was nominated for the Academy Award, BAFTA Award, Screen Actors Guild Award, and Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Actress. The film grossed over $326 million worldwide.

A sequel, The Devil Wears Prada 2, is scheduled for release in 2026.
